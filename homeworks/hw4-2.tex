\begin{problem}

  the blow-up of \(M\) at \(p\in M\), \(E=\pi^{-1}(p)\) be the exceptional divisor.
  Show that \[
    E\cdot E=-1.
  \] (\(\pi\) is defined by \(\pi(E)=\{p\}\) and \(\pi\Big|_{\tilde{M}\setminus E}\)
  from \(\tilde{M}\setminus E\) to \(M\setminus\{p\}\) is bi-holomorphism.)
\end{problem}
