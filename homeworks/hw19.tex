\begin{problem}
  At a point \(p\in M\) on a K채hler manifold \(M\), choose adapted coordinate
  around \(p\). Let \(f\) be the K채hler potential of the K채hler metric \(g\).
  Show that the curvature tensor is expressed at \(p\) by \[
    R_{i\bar{j}k\bar{l}}=\pdv{f}{z^i,\bar{z}^j,z^k,\bar{z}^l}
  .\] Use this to show \[
    R_{i\bar{j}k\bar{l}}=R_{i\bar{l}k\bar{j}}=R_{k\bar{j}i\bar{l}}
  .\] 
\end{problem}
